Syed Emad Uddin Shubha

Aspiring Researcher in Quantum Information

I completed my B.Sc. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology in May 2022. Following this, I served as a Research Assistant at North South University and attended lab courses at BRAC University. Currently, I am working as a Graduate Research Assistant at Louisiana State University. My research interests include Quantum Communication, Quantum Computing, and Quantum Information. Outside of academia, I enjoy creating memes on social issues, science, and mathematics, as well as playing chess and reading comics.
