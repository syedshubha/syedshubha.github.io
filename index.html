  • Image Encryption using XOR operation and Random Permutation Generation from password:

    This was our Numerical Techniques Sessional Course project during L2T1. MATLAB was used for this project. GitHub Link: https://github.com/syedshubha/ImageEncryption

Hobbies and Achievements

In my free time, I enjoy reading and writing about Physics and Mathematics. Despite my engineering background, I have always been fascinated by these fundamental subjects. I make an effort to learn physics and mathematics concepts beyond the engineering curriculum by studying relevant textbooks. I have dedicated significant time to understanding various topics within these disciplines.

During my freshman year of undergraduate studies, I also became involved with several science organizations. I am passionate about writing about science to foster greater public interest. I believe that a broader understanding of scientific principles and their real-world applications can contribute to a better world. Consequently, I write scientific blog posts and occasionally create YouTube videos explaining interesting concepts. I also hold the conviction that mathematics is essential for a complete understanding of science. Therefore, I emphasize the importance of mathematics education at both the high school and university levels and continuously strive to learn more mathematics myself. I firmly believe in Feynman's principle: "If you want to master something, teach it," and I actively apply this in my life.

Beyond Physics and Mathematics, my interests extend to History, Geopolitics, and more. I am a fan of comics, with The Flash (DC) and Spider-Man (Marvel) being my favorite superheroes. I am an animal lover and advocate for the philosophy of "Live and let live." In my leisure time, I enjoy creating memes and playing chess. I value both humor and chess knowledge.
